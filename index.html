Mortality in the United States:

What is killing us? And where?

CDC Mortality by Cause : 2015

What is the connection between death and geographic location in the United States? Are there patterns? Certainly, many of us have wondered if there is a connection. For example, many assume that death rates for Alzheimer's Disease would be higher in Florida, as it is a state with an older population. However, the data shows our assumptions may not always be in line with the data. Click through the dropdown below to see a choropleth map of different causes of death in 2015 for the United States.
